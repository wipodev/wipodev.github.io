### Evolution

- **Omebuddy** starts as your friendly companion.

  - Feed him **two cookies a day** â€” one in the morning and one in the evening.
  - If you forget, he transforms into **Omedweller** at noon or midnight.

- **Omedweller** is a powerful, relentless creature.

  - Hunts down the player who neglected him.
  - Disappears after one minute or upon death, allowing **Omebuddy** to return.

- Reacts differently based on the time of day.
- Nighttime increases his ambient growls and tension.
- Can destroy blocks in his path to reach the player.
